The Four Parts of Medicare
  • Part A — Hospital insurance. Free if you worked 40+ quarters (10 years)
  • Part B — Medical/outpatient. $185/month standard premium in 2025
  • Part C — Medicare Advantage. Private plans covering A+B and often D
  • Part D — Prescription drug coverage. Avg ~$55/mo
  • Medigap — Supplemental private insurance for gaps in original Medicare
Medicare Pros
Comprehensive coverage for most medical needs
Part A typically free after 40 quarters
Annual wellness visits and screenings covered
$2,000 Part D cap protects against high drug costs
Medicare Gaps
No dental, vision, or hearing in original Medicare
Late Part B enrollment = 10% penalty per missed year
IRMAA surcharges for higher-income earners
Long-term care NOT covered
